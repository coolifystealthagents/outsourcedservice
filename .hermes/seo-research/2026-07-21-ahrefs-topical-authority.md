## Commercial clusters and content gaps

1. **First handoff and 30-day launch**: chosen topic. It supports operations, admin, and customer support services and answers how the first role should work.
2. **Philippines staff outsourcing model**: compare direct hiring with provider-supported staffing. Avoid price or savings claims without current sourced evidence.
3. **Provider selection**: build on the existing provider-question route with contract ownership, worker classification, data access, quality review, and replacement questions.
4. **Work-type clusters**: operations support, admin support, customer support, and reporting/QA already have service routes. Future articles should link to one primary service rather than competing with the homepage.
5. **Offshoring vs outsourcing**: only publish if the page answers a distinct model-choice question. Do not create another broad Philippines guide.

## Internal-link plan

The rebuilt guide links to `/services/operations-support`, `/services/admin-support`, `/services/customer-support`, and `/blog/outsourced-service-provider-questions`.
